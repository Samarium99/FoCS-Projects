    Specific Heat capacity (c)

    the heat required to raise the temperature of the unit of a substance by one degree.

    • $$q_{sys} + q_{surr} = 0$$

    • Temp Changes:

      $$q = mc{\Delta}t$$

    • Phase Changes:

      $$q = m{\Delta}H = n{\Delta}H$$

    Bond Energies

    $${\Delta}H = broken - formed$$

    Heats of Formation

    $${\Delta}H = \sum{n{\Delta}H^{\circ}_{f}(products)} - \sum{n{\Delta}H^{\circ}_{f}(reactants)}$$

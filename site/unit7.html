    Solute Formulas

    Phase of matter on Vapor Pressure

    • Solid(non-volatile SOLUTES):

      reduce the solute's vapor pressure since solid solutes obstruct the surface where the solvent evaporates. $$P_{sol'n} = x_{solv} \cdot P_{solv} $$

    • Liquid(non-volatile SOLUTES):

      weighted average vapor pressure between the two liquids' vapor pressures (assuming ideal) $$P_{sol'n} = X_{a}P_{a}^{\circ} + X_{b}P_{b}^{\circ}$$

    • Gas(SOLUTES):

      amt of gas dissolved is proportional to pressure above tube. $$C = k \cdot P$$
